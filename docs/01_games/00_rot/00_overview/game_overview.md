## 1. Overview

ROT is a narrative driven survival horror game set in the coastal town of Blackwater Cove. The game focuses on exploration, environmental storytelling, and the gradual uncovering of a biological phenomenon known as the Rot.

Players explore environments that have been transformed by ecological mutation while managing limited resources and surviving encounters with organisms reshaped by the Rot ecosystem.

The experience emphasizes tension, discovery, and understanding rather than direct confrontation.

---

## 2. Genre

ROT belongs to the survival horror genre.

Key genre characteristics include:

- limited resources
- environmental exploration
- high tension encounters
- atmospheric storytelling
- player vulnerability

ROT combines traditional survival horror mechanics with ecological worldbuilding. Instead of focusing on simple infection scenarios, the game explores a biological system that restructures life and environments.

---

## 3. Core Concept

The central concept of ROT is the discovery and exploration of a biological restructuring system that transforms ecosystems.

The Rot alters organisms, environments, and ecological relationships. It does not simply infect living beings. It reorganizes them into new biological structures and ecological roles.

Players gradually uncover evidence of scientific research, failed containment efforts, and the development of Rot ecosystems throughout the world.

The game focuses on understanding the Rot rather than defeating it.

---

## 4. Player Fantasy

The player takes on the role of an individual navigating a region that has been partially transformed by the Rot.

The core player fantasy includes:

- exploring abandoned environments
- surviving encounters with mutated organisms
- uncovering hidden scientific discoveries
- learning how the Rot ecosystem functions
- piecing together the history of the outbreak

Players are not powerful heroes. They are observers and survivors attempting to navigate a world that is changing in unpredictable ways.

Success comes from careful exploration, resource management, and environmental awareness.

---

## 5. Setting Overview

ROT takes place in the coastal town of Blackwater Cove.

Blackwater Cove was once a small harbor town with fishing industries, residential neighborhoods, and research facilities established to study unusual ecological events.

Over time, signs of the Rot began to appear in the surrounding environment.

Scientific organizations attempted to investigate and contain the phenomenon. Research facilities were constructed near contaminated areas.

Eventually, parts of the region became unstable as the Rot began restructuring local ecosystems.

Evacuations occurred and many locations were abandoned.

The town now exists in a transitional state where human environments and Rot ecosystems coexist.

Players explore districts that range from lightly contaminated environments to heavily transformed Rot ecosystems.

---

## 6. Gameplay Focus

The gameplay of ROT focuses on exploration and environmental interaction.

Key gameplay elements include:

- exploring interconnected environments
- managing limited supplies
- navigating hazardous areas affected by the Rot
- encountering mutated organisms
- discovering narrative fragments through environmental storytelling

The world encourages players to investigate locations carefully and observe the environment for clues about what has occurred.

---

## 7. Narrative Approach

Storytelling in ROT is primarily delivered through environmental storytelling and fragmented narrative discovery.

Players uncover information through:

- abandoned research facilities
- scientific records
- environmental clues
- personal belongings left behind during evacuation

The narrative unfolds gradually as players explore deeper into Rot affected areas.

The full nature of the Rot is never completely explained, preserving a sense of mystery.
