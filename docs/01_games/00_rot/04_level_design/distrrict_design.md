# 1. Overview

This document defines the purpose and design role of each major district within Blackwater Cove.

Each district represents a different stage of the Rot outbreak and provides a distinct gameplay and environmental experience.

Districts should not only differ visually but also serve unique functions in terms of:

- gameplay progression  
- environmental storytelling  
- enemy encounter design  
- exploration structure  

Together, the districts form a cohesive world that gradually transitions from a recognizable coastal town into a heavily mutated Rot ecosystem.

---

# 2. District Design Philosophy

Districts in ROT are designed around several core principles.

## Environmental Identity

Each district should have a clear environmental theme.

Examples include:

- residential neighborhoods  
- commercial town centers  
- industrial harbor zones  
- medical or research facilities  

This identity helps players immediately understand where they are in the world.

---

## Gameplay Variety

Different districts should introduce new gameplay situations.

Examples include:

- narrow residential exploration  
- dense urban navigation  
- open industrial spaces  
- interior laboratory environments  

Changing environments prevents exploration from becoming repetitive.

---

## Escalating Corruption

The Rot ecosystem gradually spreads through the town.

Early districts show limited contamination, while later districts display heavy biological transformation.

This progression visually communicates the spread of the Rot.

---

# 3. Residential District

The Residential District represents the first major exploration area.

This district introduces the player to the core gameplay systems and establishes the tone of the outbreak.

## Environmental Characteristics

- small family homes  
- narrow residential streets  
- backyard fences and gardens  
- abandoned vehicles and barricades  

Rot contamination is visible but still relatively limited.

## Gameplay Role

The residential district introduces:

- basic exploration mechanics  
- early stealth encounters  
- initial combat situations  
- environmental storytelling about the outbreak

Enemy presence remains relatively low.

---

# 4. Downtown District

Downtown functions as the central hub of the game world.

This district connects multiple other districts and serves as a navigation anchor for the player.

## Environmental Characteristics

- small commercial streets  
- restaurants and shops  
- municipal buildings  
- central clock tower plaza  
- narrow alleyways connecting buildings  

## Gameplay Role

Downtown provides:

- a central navigation hub  
- access to multiple districts  
- important story discoveries  
- safe zone locations

Players revisit downtown several times as new routes become available.

---

# 5. Emergency Coordination Center

The Emergency Coordination Center represents the town’s early response to the outbreak.

Municipal buildings were converted into temporary emergency management facilities.

## Environmental Characteristics

- communication equipment rooms  
- command centers and planning maps  
- emergency supply storage  
- temporary refugee shelters  

## Gameplay Role

This district focuses on:

- puzzle based exploration  
- power restoration mechanics  
- narrative discovery about evacuation attempts

Players learn how local authorities attempted to control the outbreak.

---

# 6. Harbor District

The harbor is the economic center of Blackwater Cove.

This district represents the first area where Rot corruption becomes visibly dominant.

## Environmental Characteristics

- fishing docks  
- cargo warehouses  
- boat repair garages  
- cranes and mechanical equipment  
- industrial dock structures  

Rot growth merges with machinery and harbor infrastructure.

## Gameplay Role

The harbor introduces:

- more dangerous enemy encounters  
- vertical exploration across docks and ships  
- environmental hazards within industrial spaces

This district marks the transition from early exploration to more dangerous Rot ecosystems.

---

# 7. Rot Aquarium

The Rot Aquarium is a specialized research facility connected to marine studies.

During the outbreak, infected marine organisms escaped containment.

## Environmental Characteristics

- shattered aquarium tanks  
- flooded corridors  
- underwater observation tunnels  
- marine research laboratories  

## Gameplay Role

The aquarium introduces:

- marine based Rot organisms  
- water based environmental hazards  
- limited visibility environments

This district expands the biological scope of the Rot ecosystem.

---

# 8. Hospital District

Blackwater Hospital represents the town’s attempt to treat infected individuals.

The environment becomes increasingly claustrophobic and oppressive.

## Environmental Characteristics

- emergency wards  
- surgical theaters  
- quarantine zones  
- hospital corridors and stairwells  

Evidence of failed medical experiments and desperate treatment attempts can be found throughout the district.

## Gameplay Role

The hospital emphasizes:

- tense interior exploration  
- corridor based combat encounters  
- narrative discoveries about infection symptoms

Enemy encounters become more unpredictable.

---

# 9. Forest Region

The forest surrounds the town and forms the natural boundary of the playable world.

This district introduces large outdoor environments and wildlife mutations.

## Environmental Characteristics

- dense tree coverage  
- hiking trails  
- ranger outposts  
- abandoned logging routes  

Rot corruption spreads through roots, soil, and wildlife.

## Gameplay Role

The forest introduces:

- wildlife based enemies  
- reduced visibility environments  
- larger open exploration spaces

Players encounter stronger enemy variants in this region.

---

# 10. Fishing Trawler Graveyard

The Trawler Graveyard is a coastal setpiece environment formed by wrecked fishing vessels.

Many ships attempted to escape the harbor during the outbreak but collided along the rocky shoreline.

## Environmental Characteristics

- overturned fishing vessels  
- broken cranes  
- rusted ship hulls  
- narrow metal walkways between vessels  

Rot growth spreads across ship interiors and machinery.

## Gameplay Role

This district emphasizes:

- vertical navigation across ships  
- narrow combat environments  
- environmental hazards

The graveyard acts as a transition area toward the Halcyon research facility.

---

# 11. Halcyon Marine Research Center

The Halcyon facility is a modern research complex built on cliffs overlooking the harbor.

It secretly served as the primary location for Rot research.

## Environmental Characteristics

- marine biology laboratories  
- Rot containment chambers  
- security monitoring systems  
- underground research infrastructure  

Rot contamination spreads through the laboratory systems.

## Gameplay Role

This district reveals:

- Halcyon’s research activities  
- failed containment experiments  
- internal conflict within the organization

Enemy presence becomes significantly more dangerous.

---

# 12. Rot Convergence Chamber

The Rot Convergence Chamber lies beneath the research facility.

This subterranean location represents the core biological structure of the Rot ecosystem.

## Environmental Characteristics

- organic cavern structures  
- pulsating biological walls  
- spiral shaped Rot formations embedded in rock  

The environment appears partially ancient and partially biological.

## Gameplay Role

This district serves as the final stage of the game.

It contains:

- the most heavily corrupted environment  
- the final boss encounter  
- revelations about the Rot ecosystem

---

# 13. District Progression

Districts gradually increase in difficulty and environmental corruption.

Typical progression order:

Residential District  
Downtown District  
Emergency Coordination Center  
Harbor District  
Rot Aquarium  
Hospital District  
Forest Region  
Fishing Trawler Graveyard  
Halcyon Marine Research Center  
Rot Convergence Chamber

Each district represents a deeper stage of Rot ecosystem development.

---

# 14. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the design role of each district within Blackwater Cove.

By giving each district a clear environmental identity and gameplay purpose, the world of ROT remains varied, believable, and interconnected.

These guidelines ensure that the progression through the town reflects both narrative escalation and ecological transformation.
