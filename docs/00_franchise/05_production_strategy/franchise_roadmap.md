# Franchise Roadmap

Project: ROT  
Version: 1.1  
Document Type: Production Strategy  
Category: Franchise Planning  
Scope: ROT Universe  
Status: Core Reference  

---

## 1. Overview

The ROT franchise is designed to support multiple projects that explore different aspects of the Rot phenomenon across various locations, time periods, and narrative perspectives.

This roadmap outlines the long term direction of the franchise. It defines how the universe can expand beyond the initial game while maintaining a consistent creative identity.

The goal of the roadmap is to ensure that all future ROT projects contribute meaningfully to the development of the universe without contradicting established worldbuilding or themes.

The franchise should grow gradually through new stories, environments, and discoveries related to the Rot ecosystem.

---

## 2. Core Franchise Goals

The ROT franchise aims to achieve several long term goals.

- build a cohesive horror universe centered on ecological transformation  
- expand the mystery surrounding the Rot through new discoveries  
- explore different human responses to Rot affected environments  
- maintain consistent biological and ecological logic across all projects  

Every new project within the franchise should contribute to a deeper understanding of the Rot phenomenon while preserving its mystery.

---

## 3. Primary Game Series

The core of the franchise will be a series of narrative driven survival horror games.

Each game should explore a different region, time period, or stage of Rot ecosystem development.

These games will serve as the primary medium for expanding the ROT universe.

Possible directions for future games include:

- new outbreak zones in different geographical regions  
- large scale Rot ecosystems that have stabilized over time  
- early stage outbreaks showing the first appearance of the Rot  
- isolated research installations studying advanced Rot mutations  

Each game should reveal new information about the Rot while maintaining continuity with existing lore.

---

## 4. Expanded Lore Projects

In addition to major games, the franchise can expand through smaller narrative projects.

These projects can provide additional context for events within the ROT universe.

Examples include:

- narrative focused side stories exploring specific characters  
- short interactive experiences set within research facilities  
- story expansions that explore isolated incidents within the Rot ecosystem  

These projects allow the franchise to expand its worldbuilding without requiring large scale development.

---

## 5. Media Adaptations

The ROT universe may expand into additional forms of media that support the core narrative themes.

Possible adaptations include:

### Written Narratives

Novels or short stories that explore historical events, research discoveries, or personal accounts of Rot outbreaks.

### Visual Media

Illustrated narratives, graphic novels, or animated projects that depict environments and organisms within the Rot ecosystem.

### Documentary Style Media

In universe research recordings or investigative reports documenting Rot ecosystems and mutated organisms.

These formats can provide alternative perspectives on the ROT universe while reinforcing its scientific and ecological themes.

---

## 6. Cross Project Consistency

As the franchise expands, maintaining consistency across projects becomes increasingly important.

All new content should remain aligned with the following core elements.

- the biological logic of the Rot ecosystem  
- the visual identity of Rot environments  
- the narrative themes of ecological transformation and discovery  
- the scientific mystery surrounding the Rot  

The franchise documentation serves as the foundation that ensures all projects remain consistent with the established world.

---

## 7. Controlled Expansion

The ROT franchise should expand gradually.

New projects should introduce additional elements carefully to avoid overwhelming the core concept of the Rot.

Expansion should prioritize depth over scale.

Each new project should deepen the understanding of the Rot ecosystem rather than simply adding new creatures or environments without narrative context.

---

## 8. Long Term Narrative Development

Over time, the franchise should gradually reveal more about the Rot while preserving key unanswered questions.

Future projects may explore topics such as:

- the long term evolution of Rot ecosystems  
- advanced research into Rot biology  
- large scale societal responses to Rot affected regions  
- new environments shaped by Rot ecological dominance  

However, the ultimate origin of the Rot should remain uncertain.

Maintaining mystery is essential to the identity of the franchise.

---

## 9. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the long term direction for the ROT franchise.

It provides guidance for expanding the universe through future games, narrative projects, and media adaptations.

By following this roadmap, the ROT franchise can grow while preserving its core themes of ecological horror, biological transformation, and scientific mystery.
