## 1. Overview

The ROT universe contains several groups that interact with the phenomenon known as the Rot. These factions differ in their knowledge, goals, and methods of dealing with Rot affected environments.

Some factions attempt to understand the Rot through scientific research. Others attempt to contain it or survive within affected regions. In some cases, groups exploit the chaos created by Rot outbreaks.

This document provides a high level overview of the major factions that appear throughout the ROT universe.

These factions represent broad categories rather than rigid institutions. Individual stories may introduce specific organizations that fall within these categories.

---

## 2. The Halcyon Initiative

### Overview

The Halcyon Initiative is the most prominent research organization studying the Rot.

It was created as an international scientific program designed to investigate the biological mechanisms behind Rot ecosystems and to develop methods for containment and observation.

Halcyon facilities are often located near areas where Rot activity has been detected.

---

### Objectives

The Halcyon Initiative operates under several primary objectives.

- study the biological structure of Rot ecosystems  
- catalog mutated organisms and environmental changes  
- develop containment technologies  
- identify possible methods for controlling or stabilizing the Rot  

---

### Methods

Halcyon relies on scientific research, environmental monitoring, and experimental containment systems.

Researchers often establish controlled research zones where Rot ecosystems can be observed and studied.

These facilities frequently include laboratories, containment structures, and environmental monitoring equipment.

---

### Internal Challenges

Despite its scientific mission, the Halcyon Initiative faces several internal challenges.

- disagreements between researchers about how the Rot should be studied  
- ethical concerns regarding experimentation on mutated organisms  
- pressure from governments seeking rapid containment solutions  

These internal conflicts occasionally lead to risky experiments or controversial research programs.

---

## 3. Local Authorities

### Overview

Local authorities represent the official institutions responsible for managing communities near Rot affected regions.

These groups include local governments, emergency services, and regional administrative organizations.

Their primary responsibility is maintaining public safety.

---

### Responsibilities

Local authorities attempt to manage the immediate consequences of Rot activity.

Common responsibilities include:

- evacuation of civilians from affected areas  
- enforcement of quarantine zones  
- coordination with research organizations  
- maintaining basic infrastructure and communication  

---

### Limitations

Local authorities often lack the resources and knowledge required to effectively manage Rot ecosystems.

Their strategies typically focus on containment and evacuation rather than scientific investigation.

As Rot activity increases, many local authorities lose control of affected regions.

---

## 4. Civilian Groups

### Overview

Civilian groups consist of ordinary people attempting to survive in areas affected by the Rot.

These groups vary widely in structure and organization.

Some remain small and isolated, while others develop into larger survivor communities.

---

### Motivations

Civilian groups typically prioritize survival and resource gathering.

Common goals include:

- securing shelter in safe locations  
- scavenging resources from abandoned areas  
- avoiding hostile organisms and dangerous environments  

---

### Knowledge of the Rot

Most civilian groups have limited understanding of the Rot.

Their knowledge often comes from observation rather than scientific study.

As a result, many survivor communities develop their own theories or myths about the Rot phenomenon.

---

## 5. Unknown Entities

### Overview

In addition to known human factions, the ROT universe contains entities whose nature remains unclear.

These entities may represent:

- unidentified research programs  
- hidden organizations studying the Rot  
- groups attempting to exploit Rot ecosystems for unknown purposes  

---

### Observed Activity

Evidence of unknown entities sometimes appears in the form of:

- abandoned research equipment not associated with known organizations  
- unexplained containment structures  
- missing research records  
- experimental facilities with unclear origins  

These discoveries suggest that additional groups may be studying or interacting with the Rot outside official institutions.

---

## 6. Interaction Between Factions

The various factions in the ROT universe often interact in complex ways.

Research organizations rely on cooperation with local authorities to access affected areas.

Civilian groups sometimes encounter researchers while scavenging or exploring abandoned regions.

Conflicts can arise when containment operations interfere with survivor communities or when experimental research creates new environmental risks.

These interactions contribute to the tension and uncertainty that define the ROT world.

---

## 7. Purpose of This Document

This document provides a general overview of the major factions present within the ROT universe.

It establishes the roles and motivations of the groups that interact with Rot affected environments.

Individual stories or games within the franchise may introduce specific organizations or characters that belong to these faction categories.
