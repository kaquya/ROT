# 1. Overview

Setpiece locations are major scripted environments within ROT that provide memorable moments during exploration.

These locations are designed to deliver strong visual identity, narrative significance, and unique gameplay situations. Setpieces often contain important encounters, environmental storytelling, or major progression events.

Unlike standard exploration areas, setpieces are carefully staged environments where level design, audio, lighting, and encounter design work together to create a distinct experience.

Setpiece locations help define the identity of Blackwater Cove and make specific areas of the world stand out.

---

# 2. Setpiece Design Philosophy

Setpieces follow several key design principles.

## Strong Visual Identity

Each setpiece location should be visually distinct and recognizable.

Examples include:

- large environmental landmarks  
- unusual architectural structures  
- dramatic environmental damage  
- unique Rot growth patterns

Players should immediately recognize when they have entered a major location.

---

## Narrative Significance

Setpiece locations should contribute to the story of the world.

These locations often represent important moments in the outbreak or reveal key information about the Rot ecosystem.

Examples include:

- locations where major events occurred  
- research facilities containing critical information  
- environments showing the scale of Rot transformation

---

## Gameplay Variety

Setpieces should introduce gameplay situations that differ from typical exploration areas.

Examples include:

- major combat encounters  
- environmental hazards  
- puzzle sequences  
- vertical exploration spaces

Setpieces help break up exploration with memorable gameplay moments.

---

## Environmental Storytelling

The environment itself should communicate the history of the location.

Examples include:

- signs of evacuation attempts  
- damaged infrastructure  
- scientific experiments gone wrong  
- evidence of Rot ecosystem growth

Players should be able to understand what happened in a location through observation.

---

# 3. Major Setpiece Locations

The following locations represent major scripted environments within the game world.

---

## Collapsed Lighthouse

### Location

Coastal cliffs overlooking the harbor.

### Description

The lighthouse once guided ships safely into the harbor of Blackwater Cove.

During the outbreak, structural damage and Rot growth caused part of the tower to collapse.

The remaining structure still stands as one of the tallest landmarks in the region.

### Gameplay Role

- navigation landmark visible from multiple districts  
- environmental storytelling about early evacuation attempts  
- exploration of the collapsed interior

The lighthouse provides a dramatic visual anchor for the coastal environment.

---

## Rot Aquarium

### Location

Harbor coastline near the marine research facilities.

### Description

Originally built as a public marine exhibit and research center, the aquarium was later used by Halcyon to study unusual marine organisms.

During the outbreak, several tanks ruptured, releasing contaminated marine life.

Large sections of the structure are now flooded and overtaken by Rot growth.

### Gameplay Role

- introduction of marine Rot organisms  
- limited visibility combat areas  
- flooded corridors and environmental hazards

The aquarium serves as one of the most visually distinct environments in the game.

---

## Fishing Trawler Graveyard

### Location

Rocky shoreline beyond the harbor district.

### Description

At the beginning of the outbreak, multiple fishing vessels attempted to flee Blackwater Cove.

Several ships collided in the chaos and ran aground along the coast.

The wreckage now forms a maze of rusted metal structures covered in Rot biomass.

### Gameplay Role

- vertical exploration across ship decks and cranes  
- narrow combat spaces  
- environmental hazards such as unstable walkways

The graveyard visually represents the desperate attempts to escape the town.

---

## Downtown Clock Tower Plaza

### Location

Central downtown district.

### Description

The clock tower stands at the center of the town plaza and serves as a major urban landmark.

During the outbreak the plaza became a temporary evacuation gathering point.

Emergency vehicles and barricades remain scattered throughout the area.

### Gameplay Role

- central navigation landmark  
- major encounter space  
- environmental storytelling about evacuation failure

The plaza acts as a recognizable central hub for the town.

---

## Blackwater Hospital

### Location

Hospital district near the edge of downtown.

### Description

Blackwater Hospital became the primary medical response center during the early stages of the outbreak.

As the situation worsened, the facility was overwhelmed with infected patients and emergency cases.

Sections of the hospital show evidence of desperate treatment attempts and failed containment efforts.

### Gameplay Role

- tense interior exploration environment  
- narrative discoveries about infection symptoms  
- major boss encounter

The hospital emphasizes claustrophobic horror and escalating danger.

---

## Halcyon Marine Research Center

### Location

Cliffs overlooking the harbor.

### Description

The Halcyon facility is a modern research complex built to study unusual marine biology.

In reality, the facility secretly investigated the Rot ecosystem and conducted biological experiments.

The research center reveals the true scope of Halcyon's involvement in the events of Blackwater Cove.

### Gameplay Role

- late game exploration area  
- discovery of major narrative revelations  
- high Rot contamination zones

This location connects the surface world to the deeper Rot structures beneath the town.

---

## Rot Convergence Chamber

### Location

Subterranean caverns beneath the Halcyon research facility.

### Description

The convergence chamber is a massive underground biological structure where Rot growth appears to originate.

The environment resembles a fusion of organic tissue and ancient geological formations.

The chamber suggests that the Rot ecosystem existed beneath the region long before modern research began.

### Gameplay Role

- final area of the game  
- heavily corrupted environment  
- final boss encounter

This location represents the ultimate transformation of the environment by the Rot ecosystem.

---

# 4. Setpiece Integration

Setpiece locations should be integrated naturally into the world map.

Players should encounter these areas through exploration rather than abrupt transitions.

Each setpiece should:

- connect logically to surrounding districts  
- provide strong environmental storytelling  
- support memorable gameplay events

Setpieces should enhance the feeling that Blackwater Cove is a real place undergoing catastrophic transformation.

---

# 5. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the major setpiece locations within ROT.

These locations provide some of the most memorable environments in the game world and support key narrative and gameplay moments.

By designing visually distinct and narratively meaningful locations, setpieces help shape the identity of Blackwater Cove and reinforce the ecological horror of the Rot ecosystem.
