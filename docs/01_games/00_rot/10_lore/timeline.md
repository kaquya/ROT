# 1. Overview

This document defines the historical timeline of Blackwater Cove and the major events that led to the Rot outbreak.

The timeline provides narrative context for environmental storytelling, documents discovered by the player, and the collapse of the town.

Events are divided into several periods:

- Early settlement of Blackwater Cove  
- Industrial and scientific development  
- Discovery of the Rot ecosystem  
- Halcyon research expansion  
- The outbreak and collapse of the town  

This timeline provides the foundation for all narrative content in ROT.

---

# 2. Early Settlement (Late 1800s)

Blackwater Cove began as a small fishing settlement along a rugged northern coastline.

The region’s natural harbor made it ideal for maritime trade and fishing activity.

Early settlers established:

- small fishing docks
- basic residential housing
- a lighthouse overlooking the harbor cliffs

The town remained relatively isolated due to difficult terrain and limited road access.

The surrounding forests and coastal cliffs made large-scale expansion difficult.

For decades the community remained small and economically dependent on fishing.

---

# 3. Growth of the Fishing Industry (1920–1960)

During the early twentieth century the fishing industry expanded.

New infrastructure was built including:

- larger dock systems
- boat repair facilities
- fish processing warehouses
- expanded residential districts

The harbor became the economic center of the town.

Commercial fishing vessels began operating deeper into the surrounding ocean trench systems.

The town slowly developed into a small but stable coastal community.

---

# 4. Early Scientific Interest (1970–1990)

During the late twentieth century marine researchers began studying unusual biological activity in the waters near Blackwater Cove.

Researchers reported strange biological formations in deep ocean trenches near the coast.

Initial discoveries included:

- unusual coral-like growths
- abnormal marine organisms
- unexplained biological restructuring in deep sea samples

These discoveries attracted attention from private research organizations.

---

# 5. Halcyon Initiative Arrival (1998)

The Halcyon Initiative established a marine research facility near Blackwater Cove.

Publicly, the organization presented itself as a marine biology research group.

Privately, Halcyon focused on studying unusual biological structures discovered in the nearby ocean trench.

The research facility was constructed on cliffs overlooking the harbor.

This facility would later become central to the Rot research program.

---

# 6. Discovery of the Rot Organism (2004)

Halcyon researchers recovered deep ocean biological samples from trench systems offshore.

The samples contained a previously unknown biological structure.

This organism displayed unusual properties.

Observations included:

- rapid cellular restructuring
- adaptive biological growth
- integration with surrounding organic material

Researchers initially believed the organism represented a new form of marine life.

Further study revealed something far more unusual.

---

# 7. Early Rot Research (2005–2010)

Halcyon began classified research into the organism's properties.

Scientists discovered that the organism could restructure biological tissue rather than simply infect it.

Early experiments demonstrated:

- integration with plant life
- mutation of marine organisms
- rapid structural growth

The organism appeared capable of reorganizing biological systems into new ecological forms.

This phenomenon was eventually referred to internally as **the Rot**.

---

# 8. Containment Experiments (2011–2016)

Halcyon expanded its research operations.

The facility constructed underground laboratories designed to contain Rot samples.

Research experiments attempted to study:

- Rot growth patterns
- biological restructuring processes
- potential energy applications

Several internal reports warned that the organism was far more complex than originally believed.

Some researchers began questioning whether the Rot was truly an organism at all.

---

# 9. Increasing Anomalies (2017)

Unusual biological incidents began occurring within research laboratories.

Examples included:

- uncontrolled growth of Rot biomass
- mutations in experimental animals
- unexplained contamination in facility systems

Halcyon continued its research despite internal warnings.

Official reports downplayed the severity of these events.

---

# 10. Marine Ecosystem Changes (2018)

Fishermen began reporting strange changes in marine life near the harbor.

Observed phenomena included:

- malformed fish
- unusual coral-like structures attached to vessels
- strange biological formations in fishing nets

Local authorities dismissed these reports as natural anomalies.

However, Halcyon researchers privately recognized these as signs of Rot spread.

---

# 11. First Containment Failure (2019)

A containment failure occurred within the Halcyon research facility.

Rot growth escaped a laboratory containment chamber.

While the incident was reportedly contained, the organism began spreading through underground systems beneath the facility.

Researchers began discovering Rot structures integrated into rock formations below the laboratory.

---

# 12. Early Infection of Local Wildlife (2020)

Mutated wildlife began appearing in the surrounding forests.

Examples included:

- aggressive animal behavior
- visible biological deformities
- unusual Rot growth structures in soil

These incidents were rarely reported outside the region.

Local authorities lacked the resources to investigate the phenomenon properly.

---

# 13. Expansion of Rot Ecosystem (2021)

The Rot ecosystem began expanding rapidly beneath the town.

Subterranean growth structures developed within underground caverns connected to the ocean trench.

These structures appeared ancient and massive in scale.

Some researchers theorized that the Rot ecosystem had existed beneath the region long before human discovery.

---

# 14. Halcyon Internal Conflict (Early 2022)

Several researchers within Halcyon attempted to halt the ongoing experiments.

They believed the organism could not be controlled safely.

Other members of the organization insisted that the Rot represented a breakthrough biological discovery.

This disagreement created divisions within the research team.

Despite the warnings, research continued.

---

# 15. Initial Outbreak (Mid 2022)

The Rot spread beyond the research facility.

Early contamination appeared within:

- harbor infrastructure
- coastal structures
- underground tunnels

Rot organisms began integrating with marine life and coastal environments.

The first infected creatures appeared near the harbor district.

---

# 16. Collapse of Local Containment (Late 2022)

Local authorities attempted to respond to the situation.

Emergency operations were established in the downtown district.

However, the outbreak spread faster than expected.

Infrastructure failures began occurring throughout the town.

Residents attempted to evacuate, but transportation routes quickly became blocked.

---

# 17. Harbor Disaster

During evacuation attempts, many fishing vessels attempted to leave the harbor.

Several ships collided during the chaos.

Many vessels were abandoned or wrecked along the coastline.

These ships later formed the **Fishing Trawler Graveyard**.

Rot growth quickly spread across the wreckage.

---

# 18. Hospital Crisis

The local hospital became overwhelmed with infected residents.

Medical staff attempted to treat infected patients.

However, the Rot did not behave like a traditional disease.

Patients experienced severe biological restructuring.

Quarantine efforts failed.

The hospital became one of the most heavily contaminated areas in the town.

---

# 19. Emergency Coordination Center

Local authorities attempted to organize a response from municipal buildings.

An emergency command center was established.

Attempts were made to:

- organize evacuation routes
- restore communications
- establish quarantine zones

These efforts ultimately failed as the outbreak continued spreading.

---

# 20. Town Collapse

Within a short period, Blackwater Cove became largely abandoned.

Survivors fled or disappeared.

Rot growth began transforming the environment itself.

Buildings, infrastructure, and natural terrain began merging with biological growth structures.

The town gradually became part of a living ecosystem.

---

# 21. Current State of Blackwater Cove

By the time the events of ROT take place, Blackwater Cove is almost completely overrun.

The environment now contains:

- mutated wildlife
- Rot growth structures
- abandoned infrastructure
- remnants of the town's final days

The Halcyon Research Facility remains at the center of the outbreak.

Beneath it lies the **Rot Convergence Chamber**, the heart of the organism’s growth beneath the town.

---

# 22. Purpose of This Document

This document provides the narrative timeline of Blackwater Cove and the events leading to the Rot outbreak.

The timeline serves as a reference for:

- environmental storytelling
- collectible documents
- narrative design
- level design context

Understanding the history of the town helps ensure that all narrative elements remain consistent throughout the game world.
