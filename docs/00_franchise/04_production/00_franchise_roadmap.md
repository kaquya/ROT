# ROT – Franchise Roadmap

Version: 1.0  
Status: Long-Term Franchise Planning

---

# 1. Purpose of This Document

The Franchise Roadmap defines the long-term vision of the ROT universe.

While the first ROT game focuses on a single outbreak location, the broader franchise is designed to expand across multiple environments and storylines.

This document outlines:

• the long-term narrative direction of the franchise  
• how future games expand the Rot mystery  
• potential settings for future outbreak zones  
• the evolution of gameplay and world scope  

The roadmap ensures the ROT universe can grow while maintaining narrative consistency.

---

# 2. Franchise Structure

The ROT franchise is structured as a series of self-contained survival horror experiences.

Each game focuses on a different outbreak zone and a different protagonist.

While the individual stories are largely self-contained, each entry reveals new information about the Rot phenomenon.

This approach allows the universe to expand gradually.

Players should feel that each game reveals a new piece of a larger mystery.

---

# 3. Narrative Expansion Model

The ROT narrative expands through three major phases.

Each phase gradually increases the player’s understanding of the Rot system.

---

## Phase 1 – Localized Outbreaks

Early entries in the franchise focus on isolated outbreak zones.

These locations are geographically contained and disconnected from the outside world.

Players experience the disaster from the perspective of individuals trapped inside the environment.

Examples include:

• coastal towns  
• research facilities  
• small isolated communities  

The focus is on survival and discovery.

The broader nature of the Rot remains mysterious.

---

## Phase 2 – Regional Spread

Later entries reveal that the Rot phenomenon is not limited to a single location.

Evidence begins to appear showing that similar outbreaks have occurred in other regions.

Players begin to uncover connections between these events.

New narrative elements may include:

• global research programs  
• hidden Rot research sites  
• containment operations  

At this stage the Rot begins to appear as a larger ecological system rather than a single disaster.

---

## Phase 3 – Planetary System

The final stage of the franchise explores the possibility that the Rot represents a planetary-scale biological infrastructure.

Evidence suggests that the Rot may have existed long before human civilization.

Future stories may explore:

• ancient geological Rot structures  
• deep ocean ecosystems  
• prehistoric biological archives  

At this stage the narrative shifts from survival horror to existential discovery.

---

# 5. Franchise Gameplay Evolution

Each ROT game may introduce new gameplay systems while maintaining the core survival horror foundation.

Possible gameplay expansions include:

• new enemy ecosystems  
• expanded environmental interaction  
• advanced Rot growth mechanics  
• environmental survival hazards

However, the core gameplay identity should remain consistent across the franchise.

---

# 6. Long-Term Narrative Mysteries

The ROT franchise is built around a set of long-term mysteries that gradually unfold across multiple entries.

These mysteries should never be answered too quickly.

Instead, each game should reveal small fragments of information that expand the player’s understanding of the Rot phenomenon.

Key mysteries include:

• the true origin of the Rot  
• how long the Rot has existed on Earth  
• whether the Rot is a natural planetary system  
• why certain environments trigger Rot activation  
• whether the Rot can be controlled or influenced  

Maintaining these mysteries helps preserve narrative tension across the franchise.

---

# 7. Halcyon Initiative Narrative Arc

The Halcyon Initiative plays a central role in the ROT universe.

Across multiple entries, the player gradually learns more about the organization.

---

## 7.1 Early Research

Early entries in the franchise portray Halcyon as a scientific organization attempting to study unusual biological discoveries.

Researchers initially believe the Rot may offer revolutionary ecological benefits.

Halcyon facilities operate under the belief that the organism can be controlled.

---

## 7.2 Internal Division

As research progresses, divisions appear within the organization.

Some scientists argue that the Rot represents a planetary recovery system.

Others believe the organism poses an existential threat.

These disagreements lead to controversial research decisions.

---

## 7.3 Collapse of Containment

Over time, Halcyon experiments increasingly fail to contain the Rot ecosystem.

Facilities experience containment breaches.

Research operations begin to collapse.

Some members of the organization begin advocating cooperation with the Rot rather than resistance.

---

## 7.4 Future Narrative Possibilities

Later franchise entries may reveal that Halcyon research continues in hidden facilities.

Certain researchers may believe humanity must adapt to the Rot ecosystem rather than attempting to destroy it.

This creates narrative tension between:

• containment advocates  
• ecological integration advocates

---

# 8. Global Rot Discovery Arc

As the franchise expands, players gradually discover that the Rot is not limited to isolated outbreaks.

Evidence may emerge suggesting that the Rot exists across multiple regions.

Examples include:

• ancient Rot biological structures  
• deep ocean trench ecosystems  
• underground cave networks containing Rot tissue

These discoveries suggest that the Rot has existed for far longer than human civilization.

# 11. Franchise Design Rules

To maintain consistency across future entries, several rules should always be followed.

---

## 11.1 Each Game Focuses on One Outbreak Zone

Each ROT game should center on a specific location experiencing a Rot activation.

This allows environments to remain detailed and immersive.

---

## 11.2 The Rot Remains Mysterious

The full nature of the Rot should never be fully explained.

Even at the end of the franchise, certain aspects should remain unknown.

---

## 11.3 Gameplay Remains Survival Horror

While gameplay may evolve, ROT should always retain its survival horror identity.

The player should remain vulnerable.

Resource scarcity should remain a core mechanic.

---

## 11.4 Environments Drive the Narrative

The environment must remain the primary storytelling medium.

Dialogue and cutscenes should support the environment rather than replace it.

---

# 12. Long-Term Franchise Vision

The ultimate goal of the ROT franchise is to explore the relationship between humanity and the biological systems of the planet.

What begins as a local survival horror story gradually expands into a larger question:

What if humanity is not the dominant biological system on Earth?

The Rot may represent a natural process that existed long before human civilization.

The franchise explores the consequences of discovering such a system.
