# 1. Overview

This document defines the overall structure of the playable world in ROT.

The game takes place in the coastal town of **Blackwater Cove**, a small maritime settlement gradually transformed by the Rot ecosystem.

The purpose of this document is to describe:

- the geographic layout of the region
- the major districts of the town
- how locations connect to each other
- the overall exploration structure of the game world

Detailed internal layouts for each district are defined in `district_layouts.md`.

The world should feel like a **coherent physical location** rather than a collection of disconnected levels.

---

# 2. Blackwater Cove Overview

Blackwater Cove is located along a rugged northern coastline.

The town originally developed as a fishing community centered around a natural harbor. Over time it expanded with residential neighborhoods, municipal infrastructure, and research facilities studying unusual ecological phenomena.

The surrounding geography isolates the town from major population centers.

Natural boundaries include:

- steep coastal cliffs
- dense evergreen forest
- rocky shoreline
- unstable coastal roads
- deep ocean trenches offshore

These geographic features explain why the Rot outbreak initially remained contained within the region.

---

# 3. Geographic Regions

The Blackwater Cove area can be divided into three broad environmental regions.

---

## Coastal Zone

The coastal zone includes the harbor and surrounding marine infrastructure.

Locations include:

- fishing docks
- boat repair yards
- cargo warehouses
- lighthouse cliffs
- coastal research facilities

Rot corruption spreads rapidly in this area due to marine contamination.

---

## Urban Zone

The urban zone contains the main town center and residential districts.

This region includes:

- residential neighborhoods
- downtown commercial areas
- municipal buildings
- emergency response infrastructure

The early stages of the outbreak occur in this area.

---

## Forest Zone

The surrounding forest forms the outer boundary of the playable world.

Features include:

- hiking trails
- ranger stations
- abandoned logging roads
- steep terrain

As the game progresses, the forest becomes increasingly corrupted by Rot ecosystem growth.

---

# 4. Town Structure

Blackwater Cove developed gradually over several decades.

Unlike modern planned cities, the town has an irregular layout.

Common characteristics include:

- narrow coastal roads
- dense residential blocks
- small commercial streets
- layered infrastructure built over older structures

This organic layout supports exploration and reinforces the sense of a real location.

Players gradually learn the geography of the town through repeated traversal.

---

# 5. Major Districts

The playable world is divided into several major districts.

Each district represents a different stage of Rot contamination and environmental transformation.

Primary districts include:

- Residential District
- Downtown District
- Emergency Coordination Center
- Harbor District
- Rot Aquarium
- Hospital District
- Forest Region
- Fishing Trawler Graveyard
- Halcyon Marine Research Center
- Rot Convergence Chamber

Players gradually unlock these areas as the story progresses.

---

# 6. Setpiece Locations

Several major environmental landmarks provide unique visual identity and exploration experiences.

---

## Collapsed Lighthouse

A lighthouse overlooking the harbor cliffs.

The structure partially collapsed during the outbreak as Rot growth integrated with the foundation.

The remaining tower is visible from multiple districts and serves as a major navigation landmark.

---

## Rot Aquarium

A flooded marine research facility used by Halcyon to study oceanic biological anomalies.

The structure contains shattered tanks and contaminated marine organisms.

This location introduces marine based Rot mutations.

---

## Fishing Trawler Graveyard

A cluster of wrecked fishing vessels along the rocky coastline.

Many ships attempted to flee the harbor during the outbreak and collided in the process.

The wreckage forms a maze of ships and rusted metal structures.

Rot growth has spread throughout the vessels.

---

# 7. World Connection Structure

Downtown acts as the central navigation hub of the game world.

Most districts connect through this location.

Example world structure:

Forest Region  
│  
Collapsed Lighthouse  
│  
Residential District  
│  
Downtown Hub  
│  
Emergency Center – Hospital – Harbor District  
│  
Rot Aquarium  
│  
Trawler Graveyard  
│  
Halcyon Research Center  
│  
Rot Convergence Chamber

This structure ensures:

- a recognizable central hub
- multiple exploration routes
- logical geographic relationships

---

# 8. Exploration Philosophy

The world map is designed around exploration loops rather than linear progression.

Each district contains:

- multiple entry routes
- environmental shortcuts
- optional exploration areas
- narrative discovery locations

Players gradually unlock shortcuts between districts, allowing faster movement through the town.

Navigation relies on environmental familiarity rather than a traditional minimap.

---

# 9. Navigation Landmarks

Several large structures help players orient themselves.

These landmarks are visible across multiple districts.

Major landmarks include:

Harbor Lighthouse  
Downtown Clock Tower  
Blackwater Hospital Tower  
Halcyon Research Facility

These landmarks act as natural navigation anchors within the world.

---

# 10. World Boundaries

The playable world is contained using natural environmental barriers.

Examples include:

- collapsed roads
- landslides
- destroyed bridges
- blocked tunnels
- cliff edges
- dense Rot biomass barriers

These barriers prevent exploration outside the intended play space without breaking immersion.

---

# 11. District Progression

Players gradually unlock districts as the story progresses.

Typical progression order:

Residential District  
Downtown District  
Emergency Coordination Center  
Harbor District  
Rot Aquarium  
Hospital District  
Forest Region  
Fishing Trawler Graveyard  
Halcyon Research Center  
Rot Convergence Chamber

Although progression follows this order, players often revisit earlier areas using newly unlocked shortcuts.

---

# 12. World Design Goals

The world design of ROT aims to achieve several goals.

- create a believable coastal town environment
- support exploration driven gameplay
- deliver environmental storytelling
- gradually escalate ecological horror
- connect districts through logical geography

Blackwater Cove should begin as a recognizable town but slowly transform into a biological ecosystem dominated by the Rot.
