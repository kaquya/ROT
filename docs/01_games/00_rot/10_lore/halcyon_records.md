# 1. Overview

This document defines the internal records of the Halcyon Initiative that appear throughout the game.

Halcyon records are collectible narrative fragments discovered by the player during exploration. These documents reveal the hidden research conducted by the Halcyon Initiative and gradually expose the truth behind the Rot ecosystem.

The records are written in several formats including:

- scientific research notes  
- internal communications  
- experiment reports  
- incident logs  
- personal researcher journals  

Together these documents allow the player to reconstruct the events leading to the collapse of Blackwater Cove.

---

# 2. Purpose of Halcyon Records

Halcyon documents serve several narrative purposes.

First, they reveal the scientific investigation into the Rot organism.

Second, they expose the internal conflicts within the Halcyon Initiative.

Third, they gradually uncover the truth about the Rot ecosystem beneath the town.

Rather than presenting exposition through cutscenes, the narrative is discovered through exploration and document collection.

---

# 3. Document Categories

Halcyon records are divided into several categories.

## Research Logs

Research logs contain scientific observations recorded by Halcyon researchers.

These documents describe:

- Rot biological structure  
- mutation behavior  
- containment experiments  
- ecological analysis  

Research logs often contain technical language and fragmented data.

---

## Incident Reports

Incident reports describe containment failures and laboratory accidents.

Examples include:

- uncontrolled Rot growth  
- infected experimental organisms  
- laboratory breaches  

These reports gradually reveal how dangerous the organism became during research.

---

## Internal Communications

Internal communications include messages exchanged between Halcyon personnel.

These records may appear as:

- internal emails  
- recorded voice logs  
- written memos  

These documents reveal internal disagreements among researchers regarding the ethics and safety of continued experimentation.

---

## Personal Journals

Some researchers kept private notes documenting their concerns and observations.

These journals provide a more emotional perspective on the research.

Personal entries often describe:

- fear regarding the organism  
- ethical concerns  
- growing distrust of Halcyon leadership  

These documents humanize the scientists involved in the project.

---

## Containment Logs

Containment logs document the status of Rot samples held within laboratory environments.

These logs record:

- growth activity  
- containment stability  
- experimental procedures  

Later entries often reveal the gradual loss of control over the organism.

---

# 4. Narrative Structure

Halcyon records are discovered gradually throughout the game.

Early documents focus on:

- scientific curiosity  
- early research optimism  
- limited understanding of the organism

Mid-game documents reveal:

- containment failures  
- mutated organisms  
- expanding research experiments

Late-game documents expose:

- the true scale of the Rot ecosystem  
- the ethical collapse of Halcyon research  
- the final attempts to understand the organism.

---

# 5. Key Researchers

Several researchers appear frequently in Halcyon records.

These recurring figures help connect multiple documents into a coherent narrative.

Examples include:

Lead Researcher  
Responsible for overseeing the Rot study program.

Marine Biology Specialist  
Initially responsible for analyzing marine samples recovered from deep ocean trenches.

Containment Engineer  
Responsible for maintaining the facility’s biological containment systems.

Field Research Assistant  
One of the first individuals to document unusual environmental effects outside the facility.

Some of these researchers disappear from later records as the situation deteriorates.

---

# 6. Document Discovery

Halcyon records appear in several locations across the game world.

Examples include:

- research laboratories  
- office workspaces  
- containment chambers  
- data archives  
- personal quarters of researchers  

Document placement supports environmental storytelling by appearing in logical locations.

---

# 7. Audio Log Variants

Some Halcyon records may appear as audio recordings rather than written documents.

Audio logs allow players to hear the voices of researchers describing events directly.

These recordings often capture moments of panic or confusion during containment failures.

---

# 8. Player Archive System

Collected Halcyon records are stored within the player's archive.

The archive allows players to:

- review previously discovered documents  
- track missing records  
- revisit important narrative clues

The archive also organizes documents chronologically when possible.

---

# 9. Tone and Writing Style

Halcyon records follow a consistent writing style.

Scientific documents use formal language and structured reporting.

Personal journals use more emotional and subjective writing.

Later documents become increasingly fragmented and urgent as the situation deteriorates.

This progression reinforces the collapse of the research program.

---

# 10. Narrative Themes

The Halcyon records explore several key themes.

These include:

- scientific curiosity and hubris  
- the ethical consequences of unchecked experimentation  
- humanity’s inability to fully understand alien biological systems  

The documents suggest that the Rot ecosystem may be far older and more complex than Halcyon researchers initially believed.

---

# 11. Integration with Gameplay

Halcyon records are integrated with several gameplay systems.

Examples include:

- collectible discovery during exploration  
- puzzle clues hidden within documents  
- narrative context for boss encounters  
- environmental storytelling elements  

Documents reward careful exploration and encourage players to investigate the environment thoroughly.

---

# 12. Purpose of This Document

This document defines the structure and role of Halcyon Initiative records within the narrative design of ROT.

These records provide the primary method through which players uncover the history of the Rot research program and the events that led to the collapse of Blackwater Cove.
