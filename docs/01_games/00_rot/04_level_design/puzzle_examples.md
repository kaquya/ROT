# 1. Overview

This document provides examples of puzzles used throughout ROT.

These examples demonstrate how puzzles should be integrated into the environment of Blackwater Cove. Each puzzle is designed to support exploration, environmental storytelling, and survival gameplay.

The puzzles described here serve as references for future level design and may be modified during development.

---

# 2. Generator Power Restoration

## Location

Emergency Coordination Center

## Description

The building's main power system has failed during the outbreak. The player must restore electricity in order to access locked security doors and communication equipment.

## Puzzle Structure

1. The player discovers that the building has no power.
2. Emergency lighting reveals a maintenance route leading to a backup generator room.
3. The generator requires fuel before it can operate.
4. The player searches nearby storage areas for fuel containers.
5. After fueling the generator, the player activates the system to restore power.

## Gameplay Purpose

- Introduces mechanical puzzle systems
- Encourages exploration of nearby rooms
- Unlocks additional areas within the facility

# 3. Downtown Security Gate

## Location

Downtown District

## Description

A barricaded security gate blocks access to a new district. Emergency authorities attempted to contain the outbreak by sealing the road.

## Puzzle Structure

1. The player encounters a locked emergency barrier.
2. A nearby control box requires a keycard to operate.
3. The keycard is found inside a nearby municipal office.
4. The player retrieves the keycard and returns to the control box.
5. The gate opens, allowing access to the next district.

## Gameplay Purpose

- Encourages exploration of surrounding buildings
- Introduces key item puzzles
- Controls world progression

# 4. Hospital Elevator System

## Location

Hospital District

## Description

The hospital elevator is nonfunctional due to power failure and mechanical damage.

The player must restore power and repair the system to access lower floors.

## Puzzle Structure

1. The elevator is discovered inoperable.
2. The player finds a maintenance note describing a backup generator in the basement.
3. The generator room is locked behind a mechanical door.
4. The player locates the necessary access key.
5. After restoring power, the elevator becomes operational.

## Gameplay Purpose

- Connects multiple floors of the hospital
- Encourages exploration of hidden maintenance areas
- Reinforces environmental storytelling

# 5. Harbor Crane Control

## Location

Harbor District

## Description

A cargo container blocks the path across a dock walkway.

The player must operate a harbor crane to move the container.

## Puzzle Structure

1. The path forward is blocked by a large cargo container.
2. A crane control cabin is located above the dock.
3. The player climbs a nearby structure to access the control cabin.
4. Inside the cabin, the player activates the crane controls.
5. The crane moves the container, clearing the path.

## Gameplay Purpose

- Introduces vertical exploration
- Uses environmental machinery as puzzle mechanics
- Creates dynamic environmental interaction

# 6. Aquarium Flood Control

## Location

Rot Aquarium

## Description

Several corridors within the aquarium are flooded and cannot be traversed safely.

The player must partially drain water from the system.

## Puzzle Structure

1. Flooded corridors block access to the research wing.
2. Maintenance logs mention a water control system.
3. The player locates the pump control room.
4. Activating the pumps reduces water levels in certain areas.
5. Previously flooded corridors become accessible.

## Gameplay Purpose

- Introduces environmental hazard puzzles
- Encourages exploration of maintenance areas
- Changes the navigable layout of the district

# 7. Forest Watchtower Signal

## Location

Forest Region

## Description

An abandoned watchtower contains an emergency signal system that can be used to unlock a sealed ranger outpost.

## Puzzle Structure

1. The ranger outpost is locked and cannot be opened manually.
2. A note indicates that the tower signal system controls emergency access locks.
3. The player climbs the watchtower.
4. Activating the signal restores power to the outpost door.
5. The outpost becomes accessible.

## Gameplay Purpose

- Encourages vertical exploration
- Rewards players who explore distant structures
- Provides narrative clues about evacuation attempts

# 8. Halcyon Laboratory Access

## Location

Halcyon Marine Research Center

## Description

Several laboratory sections are restricted behind biometric security systems.

The player must override these systems to access the facility's deeper research areas.

## Puzzle Structure

1. Laboratory doors require authorized clearance.
2. Security records reveal that emergency override systems exist.
3. The player locates a security terminal.
4. The terminal activates a temporary override sequence.
5. Restricted laboratory areas unlock.

## Gameplay Purpose

- Supports narrative discovery about Halcyon research
- Introduces late game puzzle complexity
- Unlocks key story areas

---

# 9. Multi-System Facility Power Puzzle

## Location

Halcyon Marine Research Center

## Description

The underground research elevator requires power from multiple subsystems.

## Puzzle Structure

1. The elevator lacks sufficient power.
2. The player must activate several power nodes within the facility.
3. Each node is located in a different section of the research complex.
4. Once all systems are activated, the elevator becomes operational.

## Gameplay Purpose

- Encourages exploration of the entire facility
- Serves as a late game multi-step puzzle
- Prepares the player for the final area
